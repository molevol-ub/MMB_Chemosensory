Files containing the GRs from:

- S. invicta complete GRs annotated in this protocol: GR_genomic_and_annotated_genes_GR_complete.pep.fasta
- D. melanogaster: GRs_Dmel.fasta
- A. mellifera: GRs_Amel.fasta

The sequences are concatenated in a single file to do the multiple sequence alignment
"cat GR_genomic_and_annotated_genes_GR_complete.pep.fasta GRs_Amel.fasta GRs_Dmel.fasta > GRs_allsp.fasta"

- GRs_allsp.fasta will contain the GR sequences in the three species
